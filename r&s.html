Routing & Switching

Cisco networking devices - Layer2, Layer3, Cisco Packet Tracer

I Implemented a network topology with various routing protocols which included OSPF, RIPv2, and EIGRP. These labs were done on simulation software and then deployed on real Cisco networking devices.

I also used switches to implement VLANs and switch-port security.

The largest topology I worked on contained 9 Routers, 12 Switches, 18 Hosts and 2 Servers.
